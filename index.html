My Cool Projects at Work
Vote for Better Future!
Easibook.com Pte Ltd, 2013

2013 is the year of election in Malaysia. In order to encourage the Malaysians working in Singapore to return back home for the general election, I developed online bus chartering and booking system for Malaysians to enjoy lower bus fares when they travelled back to Malaysia to cast their votes.

As a result, the general election turned out to be the highest rate that Malaysia had ever gotten in their electoral history.

First Azure Powered Vending Machines in Singapore Airport
Changi Travel Services, 2016

Working with vending machine vendors, I developed the first set of cloud-backed vending machines in Changi Airport to offer convenient ways for passengers in Transit Areas to get drinks, snacks, souvenirs, and travel essential items.

As the system is connected to Microsoft Azure, our admin staff is able to monitor the stock, transactions, machine problems via my online portal on both desktop and mobile platforms easily.
